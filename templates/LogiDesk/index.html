Welcome to your LogiDesk!

What can a LogiDesk do for you?

  • 01 Order Management

    LogiChain strives to make order flow in supply chains as seemless as possible, with a large degree of automation where safely applicable.
    With respect to order flow, each LogiDesk is additionally designed to track the progress of orders and their context, including physical items and payment.

  • 02 Supply Chain-wide Order Notifications

    LogiChain promotes transparency between all of its networks, simply because of the way transparency promotes contextual awareness, efficiency and trust.
    A LogiDesk shows all orders made between members of the supply chain's network so that future orders can be either automated or anticipated.

  • 03 Safe Payment Through Crypto

    A LogiDesk uses XRP to facilitate your orders and payments, but as far as user experience goes, the LogiDesk operates in terms of dollars, this is so that our users dont fall victim to the fluctuating prices of XRP.
    The way LogiChain operates under this condition of fluctuation is by having users buy XRP from LogiChain as they need it, LogiChain then sends XRP to the user. Once the users order is processed with their XRP, the order is shown on XRP's blockchain and the recipient is sent their XRP, LogiChain will then trade the recipient's XRP for dollars again.
    In the end, this process achieves secure and immutable payments and orders, shown to the rest of the supply chains network.
